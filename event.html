DEFECTO

This is a knowledge-based event that involves certain combination of students to acknowledge the concept identification, cause, remedy of a defect in the respective casting products. These trips the analysing skills of the students to failure analysis. Certain sets of students are appreciated to present their ideas on the failure analysis the best of the team to present a precise analysis is awarded

×

FUSE 'N' SEAL

This event aids in providing the vast knowledge on the welding parameters, the characteristics affecting the weldment, properties of the various zones, identification of the weld properties and their defects. This is a mix match event containing various rounds on correct weld parameters selection followed with successful weldment identification, followed by quality control and the team with perfect weld is awarded 3.

×

MECHASTUTE

This is a new designing event on the mnechanical components involving calculation, assumption, modelling of materials like gears, worm-wheels, shaft, spring, flywheel, couplings, bearings, and joints. Student are provided with data to design certain components, in round 1 mathematical calculations and followed with computational design of those components. The cumulative scores of each round is used to determine the winner of the event.

×

MATICIA

It deals with acknowledging the advanced Modern materials and the advancement from the normal materials. This expands the view of metallurgy from core metallurgy to material science. Student are given with modern developed materials and the convention material/ process and they are allowed to face their opponent in a debate and list the features advantaging their side that leads to the ultimate winner. This is conducted in multiple rounds within the winners of cach group.
